About us

We are two sisters, Ana and Daniela, who spend their days trying new delicious recipes. We have decided to create this blog in order to share with your our favourite ones! We called our blog Journey to Our Kitchen because we have gathered recipees from all the travels that we have done in the past years. So, we want to take you on a journey when you make one of our recipes. In the future, we would love to include videos as well so that we can cook together. Thank you for visiting!
